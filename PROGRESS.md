## 📊 Overview

**Crazy Crack** is a dynamic, high-performance, real-time multiplayer grid game based on Bingo/Loto rules with a 10×10 board layout. Players take turns calling numbers from their boards to complete lines and strike out the letters of **"CRAZY CRACK"** (10 letters total). The game supports local play against an adaptive CPU bot and full multiplayer lobby games with spectator modes, in-game chat, and synthesised audio.

### Project Statistics
* **Core Technology:** React 19, Node.js, Express, Socket.io, Capacitor
* **Completed Phases:** 4 / 5
* **Overall Progress:** 85%
